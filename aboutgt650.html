About Gt 650

RE GT650

Punchy performance: Powered by a 648cc air-cooled parallel twin engine, the GT 650 produces 47 horsepower and 52 Nm of torque, making it a thrilling ride. Modern amenities: Despite its retro looks, the GT 650 comes equipped with ABS, fuel injection, and a digital instrument cluster.
